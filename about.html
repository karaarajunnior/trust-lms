why choose us?

TLMS is a web-based platform designed to bridge the gap in accessing quality education in Uganda. It offers functionalities for students, lecturers, and administrators to manage courses, access educational resources, and monitor system usage.

Key Features:

  • Students can enroll in courses
    and download materials.
  • Lecturers can upload course materials and
    manage their courses.
  • Administrators can manage user accounts,
    configure settings, and monitor system usage.
